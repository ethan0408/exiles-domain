❓ How to Play

Exile's Domain is a strategic war game of conquest and survival.

Controls & Actions:
• Claim: Expand and claim territories (costs 100 troops, limited to 3 claims/turn).
• Attack: Invade adjacent enemy territories. Win Chance depends on terrain multipliers, troop numbers, and economic factors.
• Economy: Earn gold every turn based on territory counts. Mines generate +$15,000 but decrease happiness by 4%.
• Buildings: Build cities (increases population), forts, mines, or defensive systems (MDS) to intercept enemy missiles.
• Silos & Missiles: Build a Silo and spend $250,000 to launch missiles at enemy territories. The MDS can intercept them.
